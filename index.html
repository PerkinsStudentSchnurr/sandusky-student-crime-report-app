Sandusky Crime Report Draft

A student-created app for organizing information about a possible crime in Sandusky, Ohio. This tool creates a personal draft only; it does not send, file, or submit an official police report.

Emergency notice

If anyone is in immediate danger, call 911. Do not use this student app for emergencies, active crimes, threats, medical needs, or urgent police response.

Not a police department

This app is not associated with, endorsed by, operated by, or connected to the Sandusky Police Department, Erie County, or any legitimate police department or government agency.

Lawful use only

Use truthful information only. Ohio law prohibits knowingly false reports or false alarms to law enforcement. For non-emergency help, contact the appropriate official agency directly.

Incident Details
Include only details you reasonably believe are accurate.
Avoid guesses. Mark uncertain details as uncertain.
People, Property, and Evidence
Avoid posting private information publicly. Keep sensitive details for official agencies.
Do not alter, trespass for, or unlawfully obtain evidence.

Official contact reminder

This student app is only for drafting. For official non-emergency help, contact the appropriate local agency. Erie County lists Sandusky Police at 419-627-5863. Call 911 for emergencies.

Privacy and safety

  • Do not publish accusations, private data, or sensitive evidence online.
  • Do not confront suspects or attempt your own investigation.
  • Do not include Social Security numbers, passwords, or financial account numbers.
  • Tell an official agency if the report involves a child, weapon, injury, or ongoing threat.
